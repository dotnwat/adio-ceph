[select:no-module-found]
Open MPI was unable to find a resource allocation (llmn) component for
your environment.  The usual causes for this are an incomplete
installation of Open MPI or a batch scheduler being unexpectedly
unavailable.  In either case, you might want to check with your local
system administrator.
